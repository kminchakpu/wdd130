Exciting Expeditions

At Exciting Expeditions, our mission is to provide thrill-seekers with an unforgettable experience on the water while ensuring the highest standards of safety and environmental stewardship. We are also committed to making sure all our client are protected from dangers.

History

Founded in 1995, Exciting Expeditions started with a single raft and a passion for the river. Over the decades, we have grown into the region's premier guide service.
